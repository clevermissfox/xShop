Orders typically ship within 1-2 business days. During peak holiday season, please allow up to 3 business days for processing and shipping. You will receive an email notification with tracking information once your order has shipped.

Shipping rates vary due to the weight and distance of your order. You can always check the current rate from your cart which is calculated from the products added to your cart and the shipping address you input. Any orders $89.00 or over ship free!

Gift wrapping is a service available for most products! Fee's range from $4.99 to $29.99 and you will be able to choose from a variety of wrapping papers, gift bags, and whether to include a card! Check the option during checkout for giftwrap. Please note, gift wrapping is only available when paying with card or Paypal.

If you are not satisfied with your purchase for any reason, you can return the product within 30 days of purchase. We will provide you with a return shipping label upon your return request. You can choose to exchange the product, return it for store credit, or get a refund to your original payment method.

Please note that returned products must be unworn, in the original packaging, and with the original tags still attached. If the returned items do not satisfy these requirements, your return will not be eligible for a refund or exchange.
